Who am I?

A driven 4th year Computer Engineering student with a passion for business and technology looking to bridge the gap between first and third world countries.

Only a few months after I was born in New Jersey, I was living in Guyana (South America) until the age of ten. While growing up there, we didn't always have the latest technologies and I always found myself taking apart ancient looking computers trying to fix them. At a very young age, I knew I wanted to become an engineer through these experiences. Moving from Guyana to New York to Florida, I have met people of many different walks of life and adapted, making me a fast learner. I want to use my diverse ideas and weave them with technology to develop the next best thing.
